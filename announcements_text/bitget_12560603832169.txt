[Important] Bitget announcement: Delisting of LOKAUSDT for futures trading and futures trading bots
Bitget will delist LOKAUSDT for futures trading and futures trading bot on July 22, 2025, 3:00 PM (UTC+8). Futures Bitget will suspend the opening of new futures positions for LOKAUSDT starting from July 21, 2025, 3:00 PM (UTC+8). The order closing feature, closed orders, and TP/SL of LOKAUSDT futures will remain unaffected.Users who still hold LOKAUSDT futures positions must close them by July 22, 2025, 3:00 PM (UTC+8). After this time, Bitget will suspend futures trading for LOKAUSDT and cancel all related open orders. Any remaining positions will be settled and closed. Futures trading bots Bitget will delist LOKAUSDT for futures trading bot on July 22, 2025, 3:00 PM (UTC+8). Notes for bot users: Users should close the relevant orders in advance if they hold bot orders in these trading pairs. The publish and purchase features for the relevant bots will be suspended once the trading pairs are removed from the platform. Upon the removal, the system will automatically cancel any pending bot orders and return the relevant assets to your account. Users will still be able to view the historical data of the relevant bots after the removal. We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. Thank you for your support and understanding.
